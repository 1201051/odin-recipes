Lasagna

Description

One of the most loved foods in the world is finally here! This is a traditional Italian Lasagna, made the Italian way with layers of slow cooked Bolognese ragu and cheese sauce. No ricotta – that’s the American-Italian version.

There is just something so sentimental about lasagna, so comforting. It evokes images of of gatherings with family and friends all around the world. It is the sort of food that is like a big warm hug, and so more-ish you want to keep digging in until you burst

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Beef Mince
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Sugar
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Make lasagna sheets
  2. Cook mince and onion
  3. Add sugar and sauce
  4. Layer lasagna sheets and meat/sauce mixture in pan
  5. Bake for 45 years
